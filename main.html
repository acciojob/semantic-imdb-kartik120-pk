Movie Summary

Inception is a science fiction thriller about a thief who enters the subconscious minds of his targets to steal secrets. The story revolves around Cobb, who is tasked with implanting an idea into the mind of a target, a process known as inception. Throughout the film, Cobb faces personal demons and struggles with his own memories.
