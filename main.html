Summary

Inception (2010), directed by Christopher Nolan, is a science fiction heist thriller about a group of thieves who specialize in entering people's dreams to steal secrets from their subconscious. The story follows Dom Cobb, a thief with the rare ability to perform "extraction," the stealing of information by entering a target's dream. Cobb is offered a chance to have his past crimes erased if he can successfully perform "inception," planting an idea in someone's mind without them realizing it. As the mission becomes more complex, Cobb's own memories and guilt begin to blur the line between dream and reality.
